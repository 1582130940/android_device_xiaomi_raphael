The Xiaomi Redmi K20 Pro/Xiaomi Mi 9T Pro (codenamed _"raphael/raphaelin"_) are high-end mid-range smartphones from Xiaomi.

Xiaomi Redmi K20 Pro/Xiaomi Mi 9T Pro was announced and released in May 2019.
